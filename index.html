About

Masjid Al-Imam Al-Albani is a mosque based in Brooklyn, New York City that aims to follow the sunnah according to the understanding of the pious predecessors.

The imam of the masjid is Shaykh Abu Dujanah, who was an early student of Dammaj in Yemen and had studied with Imam Muqbil (May Allah have mercy on him) for 7 years. The shaykh currently holds lessons between Maghrib and Isha everyday.

Please note: the masjid does not have enough space to accomodate for sisters.

Lessons

Daily lessons are held at the masjid between Maghrib and 'Isha. English translators are always available and, at times, there are translators for other languages as well.

Shaykh Abu Dujanah is currently teaching the following books:

  • Kitab Al-Tawheed
  • Buloogh Al-Maraam
  • Jami' Al-Saheeh
